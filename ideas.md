Core Features
- Mood check-in (20 words): free words + optional suggested words.
- Intensity sliders: energy, stress, anxiety, joy (0–10).
- Context tags: sleep, work, social, health, weather, cycle, etc.
- Word cloud views: today, week, month, and all-time.
- Timeline: see entries on a calendar + mood trend line.
- Trigger tracking: what happened before this mood?
- Body signals: headache, tension, appetite, focus, etc.
- Coping actions: log what you did (walk, music, breathing) and if it helped.
- Reflection prompt: 1 sentence: What do I need right now?
- Micro-journal: optional short note under each check-in.

Insights
- Pattern detection: Mondays + low sleep = high stress words.
- Top changing words: words increasing/decreasing over time.
- Before/after effects: which actions improve mood in 1–3 hours.
- Weekly summary: key words, strongest emotions, best/worst days.
- Personal vocabulary: app learns your common mood language.

Engagement
- Gentle reminders: smart check-ins based on your routine.
- Streaks with meaning: consistency, not gamification pressure.
- Word goals: use 5 new emotional words this week.
- Guided emotional literacy: suggest nuanced words when user types bad/sad.

Safety + Trust
- Private-first: local storage / encryption.
- No dark patterns: no shame messaging if user misses entries.
- Crisis support shortcut: immediate help resources if high-risk words appear.
